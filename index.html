The seabed around the Vernadsky Research Base (Ukrainian Antarctic Station)

This 3D models of the seabed around the Vernadsky Research Base (Ukrainian Antarctic Station). The digital terrain models provide the basis for a comprehensive study of underwater biodiversity and support the idea of the establishment of marine protected areas in the Stella Creek and Skua Creek straits near the station «Academic Vernadsky».
